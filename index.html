Let us tell you about our expertise

Here at Bike Fixer, we will make sure to fix your bike with our utmost attention to detail and fixing quality. We are so good at fixing bikes, we guarantee that the quality of the bike will be in better shape than when you first bought it. This company was founded back in 1999 and it has grown to become one of the largest chains of bike repair services in the enitre world. We will make sure to fix up your bike and ship it back within 3-5 business days.

COVID-19: Due to the recent unfortunate pandemic situation, some of our services might be limited to local residents only. Our shop hours have been reduced to weekends only and our External Repair service is being put on hold until futher notice.
